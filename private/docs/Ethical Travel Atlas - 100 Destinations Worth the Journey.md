# Ethical Travel Atlas: 100 Destinations Worth the Journey

*An AI-Empowered Atlas for Humane Adventures, by Gyro Governance.*

One hundred globally recognizable places. This list is designed for travelers who want iconic destinations first, with practical planning and ethical context layered in.

## How to Use This Atlas

1. Open the destination you are headed to.
2. Study the adventure map and the five anchor spots first. They are designed to be the bones of any trip, short or long.
3. Read the Wikivoyage essentials so you arrive informed.
4. Apply the Ethical Compass for that destination. Each one maps to one of our four domains: Economy, Employment, Education, or Ecology.
5. Paste the AI prompt into your own assistant for a tailored plan. Then verify with locals.

## The Four Domains

| Domain | One Line | Core Question |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Economy** | Keep your spending in the places you visit. | Where does my money go? |
| **Employment** | Respect the people whose work makes travel possible. | Who benefits from my visit? |
| **Education** | Learn before you go, and learn while you are there. | Whose knowledge am I trusting? |
| **Ecology** | Leave the land and sea better than you found them. | What is my impact? |
